Advantages of Flexible Shaft Disadvantages of Flexible Shaft
A flexible shaft will give the player easy access to power without exerting much strength(little energy needed to bend the shaft)Less control of shuttle placement since the shaft is easily bent, leading to uncertainity in flight directions of the shuttlecock
Good for beginners as they can use this to practice on their technique since they won't have to focus on generating enough power for their shotsReturn of shots are slower since the shuttle stays on the string bed for a longer time due to the flex of the shaft
Can easily defend smashes since you have little time to put strength into your shotLow potential for power
Advantages of Stiff Shaft Disadvantages of Stiff Shaft
Return of shots are faster since the shuttle does not stay on the string bed as long due to stiff flex of the shaft A stiff shaft will require a stronger swing and good technique in order to generate power
More control of shuttle placement since the shaft is stiff and will get instant rebound of the shuttle Requires good use of wrist action
Higher potential powerMore suited to intermediate to advanced players
